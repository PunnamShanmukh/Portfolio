Full Description

War Ground transports players to a world ravaged by scarcity, where they must fight for survival and dominance in epic PvP battles. The game offers meticulously designed 3D environments, custom characters, and a variety of tactical gameplay mechanics, giving players the opportunity to engage in both online multiplayer and challenging offline battles with advanced AI.
